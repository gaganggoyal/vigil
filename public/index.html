Where do I find my camera's RTSP address?

Most WiFi cameras publish an RTSP link. Common formats:

  • rtsp://user:pass@IP:554/stream1 — TP-Link / Tapo, generic
  • rtsp://user:pass@IP:554/Streaming/Channels/101 — Hikvision
  • rtsp://user:pass@IP:554/cam/realmonitor?channel=1&subtype=0 — Dahua / Amcrest
  • rtsp://user:pass@IP:554/h264Preview_01_main — Reolink

Use Scan on the main screen to auto-find camera IPs on your network.
